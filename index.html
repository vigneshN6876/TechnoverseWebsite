POSTER MAKING
Rules and Regulations:

1) This is an individual event

2) A poster should be an individual effort as it is an individual event.

3) The theme of the poster will be announced on the spot.

4) Participants can use tools like Canva, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Photoshop, Visme.

5) All posters should be the original work of the participants.

6) No poster should include an image or name of government officials or popular personalities.

7) Vulgarity of any sort will lead to immediate disqualification.

8) Judge's decision will be final.
