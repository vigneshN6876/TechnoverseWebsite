Strategic Showdown
RULES :

● If participants have a chessboard, they are encouraged to bring it for the match.

● Participants will be called according to their allotted slots.

● If you lose in one round, you will be eliminated.

● If a player makes two illegal moves, they will be eliminated from the round

● The time limit for each match is 10 minutes without any increment.

● Players must use the same hand to move pieces and stop the clock.

● Once a piece is released on a square, the move is final (unless the move is illegal).

● Touch-move rule: the touched piece is mandatory to move.

● Ensure the clock is stopped before making a claim or inquiry to the arbiter.

● Participants should maintain silence in the playing hall.
